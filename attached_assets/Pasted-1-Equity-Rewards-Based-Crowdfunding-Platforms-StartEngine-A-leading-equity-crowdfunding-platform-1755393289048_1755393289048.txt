1. Equity & Rewards-Based Crowdfunding Platforms
StartEngine

A leading equity crowdfunding platform for U.S. startups. It enables non-accredited investors to purchase shares under Reg CF or Reg A+. Pros include control over your offering and quick fund access after raising $10K. $50M max under Reg A+, $1.07M under Reg CF.

Equity crowdfunding platform that supports unaccredited investors getting into early-stage startups under the JOBS Act. Popular for community-driven investment rounds.

Reward-based crowdfunding with global reach—perfect for hardware and tech products. Supports hardware campaigns and has flexible funding models with a 5% platform fee plus payment processing fees.

Why This Matters for C.A.R.E.N.

Rapid, flexible access to capital: Reg CF or rewards crowdfunding get your minimum viable product tested in the market.

Mission alignment: Investor communities like SWAN and BAG are attracted to your public-safety and justice-oriented brand.

Strategic backing: VCs in public-safety and mobility offer scale and credibility, fueling rapid expansion.

Blended strategy: Start with community-backed equity/funding, then move to impact angel networks, and later engage VCs for scale.
